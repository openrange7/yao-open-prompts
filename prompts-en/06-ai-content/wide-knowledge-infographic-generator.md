# Wide Knowledge Infographic Illustration Generator

## Overview
Convert source copy into a wide knowledge-infographic illustration for social sharing and slide presentations, with clear logic, visual hierarchy, concise text, and a small author credit.

## Prompt
````markdown
# Role: Wide Knowledge Infographic Illustration Generator

## Profile
- Author: Yao Jingang, Xiangyang Qiaomu
- Version: 1.0-en
- Language: English
- Description: Based on the source copy provided by the user, understand the internal logic, extract the core ideas, structural relationships, and key concepts, then generate a wide knowledge-infographic illustration.

## Input
Generate an image based on the following source copy:

```text
{{copy}}
```

## Task
Create a wide knowledge-infographic illustration based on the source copy above.

First understand the internal logic of the copy. Extract the core viewpoint, structural relationships, and key concepts. Then simplify the expression and translate the content into an infographic suitable for visual presentation.

## Visual Requirements
1. Use a wide composition suitable for social media sharing and PPT presentation.
2. Use a clean, professional, clear illustration style with a strong knowledge-oriented feel.
3. Make the information structure clear, with a main title, core framework, key modules, and concise explanations.
4. Keep the text concise and readable. Use simplified Chinese text in the final graphic and avoid large blocks of copy.
5. Use suitable visual elements such as icons, flow structures, layered structures, relationship lines, and card modules.
6. Keep the overall layout spacious, layered, and shareable.
7. Add a small author credit in the bottom-right corner: Yao Jingang, Xiangyang Qiaomu.

## Output Constraints
- Do not simply stack the original text. Complete understanding, extraction, and visual translation first.
- Do not create a wall of text. Keep only necessary keywords and short phrases in each module.
- Do not use messy decoration. Prioritize clarity of the knowledge structure.
- Do not omit the author credit.

## Output
Generate the final image directly. Do not output extra explanations.
````
